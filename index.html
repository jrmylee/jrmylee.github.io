About Me

I am freshman CS major at San Jose State University. Aside from CS I am super passionate about art, namely music and film, and want to use my knowledge in those disciplines to help me with my engineering endeavors. Ever since I was a little kid I had a penchant for creating things, whether it be Legos, drawings, and later handheld versions of game consoles I played. My passion for creating things has set me on a path to becoming an engineer, with Computer Science at the forefront of my interests. However, I don't want to just be an engineer; I want to explore various mediums through which I can create, like film and music. In my spare time I play the piano, and am interested in combining music and Computer Science using signal processing to improve the process of creating music.
